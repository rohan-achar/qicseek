[STW96]
M. Steiner, G. Tsudik and M. Waidner,
Diffie-Hellman Key Distribution Extended to Groups
ACM CCS'96, March 1996 
This paper introduces the group Diffie-Hellman key agreement protocols GDH.1-3 and proves their security.
[STW97]
M. Steiner, G. Tsudik and M. Waidner 
CLIQUES: A New Approach to Group Key Agreement 
IEEE ICDCS'97, May 1997 
This paper extendes STW96 to support dynamic membership (auxhiliary key agreement) operations. Protocols for join, leave, partition, and merge are defined and proven secure.
[AST98]
G. Ateniese, M. Steiner and G. Tsudik 
Authenticated Group Key Agreement and Friends 
ACM CCS'98, November 1998
A notion of group key authenitication is considered and a concrete (provably-secure) authenticated group key agreement protocol is proposed.
[ACHKT00]
G. Ateniese, O. Chevassut, D. Hasse, Y. Kim and G. Tsudik
The Design of a Group Key Agreement API
DARPA DISCEX 2000, Jan. 2000
This paper describes the Cliques GDH application protocol interface (CLQ_API) which implements GDH.2 and GDH.3 using OpenSSL. CLQ_API is independent of the underlying communication system.
[AAHKNSSST00]
Y. Amir, G. Ateniese, D. Hasse, Y. Kim, C. Nita-Rotaru, T. Schlossnagle, J. Schultz, J. Stanton and G. Tsudik
Secure Group Communication in Asynchronous Networks with Failures: Integration and Experiments, IEEE ICDCS 2000, April 2000.
This paper reports on the integration of Cliques with Spread group communication system developed at Johns Hopkins University.
[AST00]
G. Ateniese, M. Steiner and G. Tsudik 
New Multi-party Authentication Services and Key Agreement Protocols
IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communication, May 2000.
Journal version of ATW98; also defines a complete authentication protocol (strong authentication among all members).
[STW00]
M. Steiner, G. Tsudik and M. Waidner 
Key Agreement in Dynamic Peer Groups
IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems, August 2000. 
Journal version of STW96 and STW97; enhances and refines prior results.
[KPT00]
Y. Kim. A. Perrig and G. Tsudik
Simple and Fault-Tolerant Key Agreement for Dynamic Collaborative Groups, ACM CCS 2000, November 2000.
New group key agreement protocol based on Diffie-Hellman key trees. Computation complexity is reduced from O(n) to O(log n).
[ACTT01]
D. Agarwal, O. Chevassut, M. R. Thompson, and G. Tsudik
An Integrated Solution for Secure Group Communication in Wide-Area Networks, ISCC 2001, Jul. 2001.
Integration of Cliques with Totem group communication system. Authorization issues are also considered.
[KPT01]
Y. Kim. A. Perrig and G. Tsudik
Communication-Efficient Group Key Agreement, IFIP SEC 2001, Jun. 2001.
New group key agreement protocol based on Diffie-Hellman key tree. Communication cost is optimized.
[AKNT01]
Y. Amir, Y. Kim, C. Nita-Rotaru and G. Tsudik
On the Performance of Group Key Agreement Protocols, ICDCS 2002, July 2002.
Theoretical comparison and actual measurement over LAN/WAN of five group key management protocols integrated with Spread group communication system
