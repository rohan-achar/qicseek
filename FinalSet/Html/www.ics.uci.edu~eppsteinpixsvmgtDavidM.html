David Matheson



The Mathesons had returned to Palo Alto in January after living in England and Boston, so we hadn't seen them for years.
When they heard we were in town, they invited us over for a barbecue with some other old friends.
I hadn't intended to take any photos, but it turned out David had also recently gotten a digicam,
so of course we had to take them out and compare...
