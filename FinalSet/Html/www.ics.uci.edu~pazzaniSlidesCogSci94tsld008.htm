Learning from complex stimuli

  • Photographs of people doing something to a balloon

    • Action: Stretching the balloon or dipping the balloon in water
    • Color: Yellow or Purple
    • Age of Person: Adult or Child
    • (Size of Balloon: Large or Small)

  • Standard Concept Acquisition Experiment

      1. Show subject photograph

      2. Ask for category label (Alpha or not Alpha)
      3. Give correct answer
      4. Stop when six correct in a row

  • Prediction from Classic Concept Learning:

    • Disjunctive rules harder to learn than conjunctive
