Versioning and Configuration Management Working Group

  • The working group is not officially sponsored by either the IETF or the W3C
  • However, the group does have as participants, members of the following:
    • Major document management corporations (Alpharel, Documentum, Filenet, Network Imaging, Vantech, Xerox)
    • Major software config. management vendors (Atria, Continuus, MKS, P3)
    • All active hypertext versioning researchers
    • IETF HTTP WG (Larry Masinter), W3C (Dan Connolly), Netscape, Microsoft, Apache (Fielding, Thau), NTT Lab, many others...
