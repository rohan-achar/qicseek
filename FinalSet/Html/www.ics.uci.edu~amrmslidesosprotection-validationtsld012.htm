Validating Adaptation

  • Idea: Exploit additional hardware (unused gates) for additional redundancy and validation

    • Increases system reliability without major resource cost
    • High Level generation of Validation circuits; invariants, complements, etc. (automatic and manual)
    • Wide range of redundancy possible, validation can be partial to multiple in time and versions
    • Can detect and signal errors or failures
    • => May be the only approach possible for dynamic adaptation
