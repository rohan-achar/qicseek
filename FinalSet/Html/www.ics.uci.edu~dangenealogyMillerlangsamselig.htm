Hi,

Thank you again for responding to my query. I will lay out what I know.

Isidore came to the USA as an unmarried man, on 19 Oct 1906 aboard the 
ship Pretoria. His last residence (permanent) was Krakow. He was going 
to his brother Adolf (Abraham) Langsam, 127 Forsyth St, NYC. He was born 
in Krakow. Based on ship records and a letter whose details an earlier 
researcher published, Adolf, Anna, Ida, and Isidore were siblings. Their 
mother was Ruje and their father Selig, from Krakow. Isidore's Jewish 
name was Izak (Yitzchok). 

According to US vital records and the Jewish Records Indexing - Poland 
project, Izak was born July 8, 1888. More vital records data regarding 
his birth can be found on Akt 401 in film 1895667 (at a local Family 
History Center) which records Krakow Births from 1888. Let me know if 
you are interested in this. Based on his March 18, 1916 Brooklyn 
Naturalization, his wife was Jenny, born in Tarnow (now in Poland), 
listed as being 28 years old (1888?). They had two children at the time,
Ralph, born in Newark, NJ on Feb 7, 1911, and Abraham in Brooklyn on 
March 14, 1914. His occupation was "Hatter". His witnesses were Herman 
Rosenberg and Harry Kleinhandler, both hatters.

Returning to Izak's siblings - Adolf, Anna, and Ida were already mentioned. 
Ida was widowed and came to the US in 1920, age 28. her late husband's name 
seems to have been Nachemie. Another  sibling seems to have been Goldie, 
born in 1883, came to the US in 1908. Also one of Izak's sisters married 
Jacob Schultz of NY. The other Krakow birth records that seem to match in 
name or time frame are:
LANGSAM Abraham, born 1884 Akt 369 film 1895666                   
LANGSAM Chana, born 1886 Akt 291 film 1895666                   
LANGSAM Izak, born 1888 Akt 401 film 1895667                   
LANGSAM Abraham Abe, born 1889 Akt 260 film 1895667.

Isidore's brother, Adolf, married Gussie (Jewish name Malka) and had 
three children. Leo, David Schmuel and Rosalind (married to Louis 
Quaytman). David's children are Howard Earl (Jewish name Tzvi Hersh) 
and Louis (Jewish name Levi [Eli]Melech). Louis, married to Bryna 
Schneider, has two children, Michael and Elisa. In 1986 he was living 
in Seal Beach, CA.

Please share some detail regarding your own family and what you might 
know regarding Isidore (Izak)'s offspring and other siblings. 

Also of interest, do you know of any other Langsam cousins, outside of 
the offspring of Isidore (Izak) or his siblings. This will help to 
possibly connect other branches of our tree. 

I have some of this data scanned in, please let me know if I should email 
you copies of the ship manifest and naturalization.
