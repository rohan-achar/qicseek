Link Traversal

  Now you can use Chimera to traverse the Link. There are two kinds of Link traversals which will be demonstrated below. The first is one between your two open images. The next is from an open image to one that has been closed.

Traversal between open images

Once you have made anchors on bill.gif and earthrise.gif, and have connected them to the Active Link places_to_go, all you need to do to have a link traversal is to move the mouse inside the anchor area of one image and click once with the left mouse button.

Click once within the anchor area of bill.gif, and the anchor in earthrise.gif should flash like the following:

Traversal to a closed image

Jimage does not need to have an image open to respond to a link traversal. To show you that you can traverse a Link to a closed image, close the earthrise image.

To do a link traversal which will bring up the earthrise image with the anchor flashed, just click the left mouse button within the anchor area on the bill image. The earthrise image should pop up shortly with its anchor flashing in a bright color something like the following:
