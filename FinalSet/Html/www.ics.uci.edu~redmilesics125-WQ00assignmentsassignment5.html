Assignment Requirements

This assignment consists of two parts: 1) an update to the Design document (see below) and 2) a final demonstration of your system. The demonstration schedule will be posted to the Newsgroup.

Purpose and Content

The Final Document is an update of the Design Document. Some of you have been asked to modify the architecture and project-specific diagrams from your last assignment. Please do so according to the specific feedback you received from the TAs.

All of you will need to update the test plans to include resutls.

Some of you will need to include user manuals (e.g., the Athletics Helper projects).

If you are in doubt about any details, ask your TA.

All team members should be present at the final demonstration.
