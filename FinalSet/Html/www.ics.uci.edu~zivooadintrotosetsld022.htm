Software Engineering Principles

  • Software engineering principles deal with both the process of software engineering and the final product
  • A traditional problem in software engineering has been the emphasis on either the process or the product
    • both are important
  • Principles are general, abstract statements describing desirable properties of software processes and products
    • they are applicable throughout the lifecycle
  • The software engineer should still be equipped with specific methods and techniques that help incorporate the desired properties into processes and products
