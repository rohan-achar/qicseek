I never had an original letter.  A friend had a book about Jews in a small
portion of upstate NY in connection with her own family history.  She knew of
my interest in Langsams and pointed out the section.  I created a mini family
tree from the book's description.  Years later, in 1997, actually, I had a
letter from Martin Langsam, 6400 Mill Pointe Circle, Delray Beach, FL 33484 and
we carried on a minor correspondence.  He said he was the son of Jack and
Tessie Langsam from H20.  He was also related on his mother's side to H63 but I
could never get him to tell him how that relationship worked. I called him and
got very little from him.  He said that his father, Jack, was born in 1892 in
Austria.  His grandfather was Aaron or Aron.  Rose Kopita was his great aunt,
his grandfather's sister.  Aron's gravestone says that her father was Zvi
Elimelech.  Aron's wife was Etta Stecker. It was Etta who was somehow related
to H63.


Rose and Abe Kopita had a son Frank who lived in California in 1997.  He had 3
sons, but Martin didn't know their whereabouts.  Rose and Abe were married in
NYC before moving to Tupper Lake.  I never tried to find their marriage record.


Rose is probably buried in Ogdensburg.


I will make you a copy of Martin's longest letter which may help a bit more.


Suzan
