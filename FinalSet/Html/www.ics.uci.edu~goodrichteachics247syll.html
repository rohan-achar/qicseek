  • Add/drop policy. All drop requests should be given to Prof. Goodrich at office hours or by appointment. Drops will be accepted only up to the fourth week. Once your drop card has been signed, further coursework from you will not be graded. Tentative Schedule

  • Week 1:
    • Introduction. Elementary cryptography. Subsitution ciphers. One-time pad. One-way hash functions.
  • Week 2:
    • Elementary number theory. The Extended Euclid GCD Algorithm (for multiplicative inverses). Modular exponentiation. Finding prime numbers.
  • Week 3:
    • Public-key algorithms. RSA. El Gamal. Digitial Signatures.
  • Week 4:
    • Digital time stamping. Message Authentication Codes. Merkle Hash Trees.
  • Week 5:
    • Authenticated Dictionaries. RSA Accumulators. Digital certificates. Revocation.
  • Week 6:
    • Broadcast encryption. Logical Key Hierarchy (LKH). One-way Function Trees. Subset Difference Revocation.
  • Week 7:
    • Threshold cryptography and secret sharing. Searching in encrypted data.
  • Week 8:
    • Zero-knowledge proofs. Property testing. Zero-knowledge proofs of knowledge.
  • Week 9:
    • Security protocols and Oblivous transfer. Certified email. Coin flipping.
  • Week 10:
    • Electronic voting and lotteries. Temporial and Active security.
