Links

  • The Alderis project is hosted at the Center for Embedded Computer Systems, University of California, Irvine, under the supervision of Nikil Dutt.
  • We are also collaborating with researchers from the Distributed Systems Middleware Group and the DOC group at UCI, and from ISIS at Vanderbilt University.
  • The Alderis language can be analyzed using the open-source DREAM ™ tool.
  • DREAM ™ uses the UPPAAL model checker and the Verimag IF toolset for formal verification.
  • The original design of the website has been created by JayKay.
