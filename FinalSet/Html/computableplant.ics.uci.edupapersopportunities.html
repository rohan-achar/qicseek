Tenure-Track Faculty

  • Biomedical Informatics, Computational Biology, and Systems Biology Faculty  Two junior tenure-track positions are available at the University of California, Irvine in all areas of research at the intersection of life and computational sciences. These appointments will be made in the Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences with possible joint appointments in the School of Biological Sciences, the School of Physical Sciences, or the School of Medicine. Exceptionally qualified senior candidates also will be considered for tenured positions. These positions will be coordinated with the interdisciplinary research programs of the UCI Institute for Genomics and Bioinformatics. For details see Job Announcement .
