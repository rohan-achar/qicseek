Searching for Dependencies Backward Elimination and Joining

  • Wrapper Approach: Hill Climbing Search


    • Initial state: All attributes used and assumed independent
    • Operators
      • Delete an attribute
      • Join a pair of used attributes (including previously joined)
        Replaces pair with new attribute
    • Evaluation function: Accuracy as measured by leave-one-out cross validation on the training set.

  • Complexity: O(NA3)
