Taxonomy of Design Decisions

  • Latency

    • Min and max bounds, for event observation & delivery

  • Intermediation (end-to-end or relayed)

  • Delivery Constraints

    • Guarantees, transport choices, retry policies

  • Notification Management

    • Event filtering, aggregating, and queueing
    • (Un)Subscription, access control, group management
