Class Inheritance

  • A TV is a kind of home electronics appliance
    • And so are radio, VCR, CD player, LD player
  • All appliances share common properties and behavior
    • They can play audio, be turned on/off, etc.
    • They also exhibit some differences
  • One can construct a Home Electronics Appliance class
    • All appliances are derived from it
    • This is called inheritance
  • Humans are good at classifying, building taxonomies
    • Corresponds to OO inheritance hierarchies
