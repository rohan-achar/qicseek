CompSci 260 - Signing up for the 260 Portion of the Masters Exam - Winter, 2013 (Dillencourt)

The procedure for signing up for the 260 portion of the Masters Exam for Winter Quarter, 2013 is as follows:

  • If you are enrolled in the course this quarter, no additional signup is necessary.
  • If you are not enrolled in the course this quarter, send me email before Friday, February 1, as follows:
    • Put "CompSci 260 Masters Exam" in the subject line.
    • State in the body of your email that you want to take the CompSci 260 Masters exam Winter, 2013.
    • Include the following information in the body of your email:
      1. Your name
      2. Your student number
      3. The quarter(s) that you have previously taken CompSci 260.
    • Please be sure to send this to me before the deadline stated above.
    You will receive further instructions when I acknowledge receipt of your email.
