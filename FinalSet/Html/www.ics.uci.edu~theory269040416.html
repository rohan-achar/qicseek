16 Apr 2004:
Cache-Concious Sorting of Large Sets of Strings with Dynamic Tries
Josiah Carlson

The authors present an algorithm for sorting strings that betters previously known string sorting algorithms by a factor of at least 2, for large input sets. During the presentation, I'll be outlining a few often-used algorithms for string sorting, provide the algorithm designed by Sinha and Zobel, and give the findings of their experimental results.

From: Cache-Conscious Sorting of Large Sets of Strings with Dynamic Tries by Ranjan Sinha and Justin Zobel. Proceedings from the 5th Workshop on Algorithm Engineering and Experiments, 2003.
