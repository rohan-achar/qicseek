Gittel Miller's (daughter of Moshe Shamshon Scheindlin) Tombstone



Tombstone of Gittel, wife of Avrohm Miller and daughter of Moshe Shamshon Scheindlin.
This tombstone stands in Vranov nad Taplou and loosely translates from #90 on Bobby Furst's list:
Here is buried a modest God-fearing woman, her hand and heart were wide open to the poor; she suffered hardship at the end of her life, Gittel daughter of R' Moshe Shimshon of blessed memory, wife of the leader R. Avraham Miller of blessed memory, died at a ripe old age on 5 Tishrei 5689 = September 19, 1928
