On-going Homework: Team Logs

Beginning with your first team meeting!

Maintain on paper or in a file a log of team meetings that contains the following elements:

  • Team Name
  • Date
  • Time Began
  • Time Ended
  • Attendance List
  • Purpose of the Meeting
  • Minutes of the Meeting
    • Topics Discussed
    • Open Issues
    • Questions Asked/Answered
    • Individual Assignments

If two or more sub-teams meet at the same time, make separate log sheets. Log sheets are expected to be about 1 to 2 pages.
