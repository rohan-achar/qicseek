Polish Occupations

Notes

  • No diacritical marks or special Polish characters
  • Spellings changed slightly to match dictionary spellings, where appropriate
  • Occupations are from 19th century Krakow vital records

Source of English Translations

source entry meaning of source entry
dict entry translation derived exactly from the Langenscheidt Polish dictionary
dict extrapolation the exact entry did not appear in the dictionary, but there was an existing entry that could be logically modified into an occupation by following the same pattern used in other occupation translations
dict combination the phrase did not appear in the dictionary; the Polish words were looked up individually and combined
Dict surnames A Dictionary of Jewish Surnames from the Kingdom of Poland, by Alexander Beider
Jewishgen occupation not found in the dictionary; copy of the Jewishgen translation
LDS dict occupation not found in the dictionary; copy of translation from pamphlet distributed by the Latter Day Saints (Mormons)
? translation provided by the indicated source but does not match the Langenscheidt dictionary entry
blank a Krakow record indicated this occupation, but no translation could be found in any of the sources
