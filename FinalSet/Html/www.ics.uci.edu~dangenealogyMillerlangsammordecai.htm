The Mordechai Langsam Family


Mordechai Langsam was known to have been a descendent of the Bnei Yissoschur, R' Hersh Mylech Spira. This most likely places this branch in the L5 section of the "Langsam-Spira Family Tree" book, as many of those Langsams claim descent via both Chaim Leib Langsam and his brother, the Bnei Yissoschur. Mordechai had one known son. According to Mordechay (Moti) Pollack, Mordechai Langsam (1878 - 1953) was from Lesko, a son of Joseph, son of Yona Langsam. See his email describing this.
