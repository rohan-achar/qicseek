Riptide vs. Silver Bullets

Riptide started hot, quickly building up a 10-0 lead, enough to overcome a late rally by the Silver Bullets and win 10-5. The Silver Bullets were Riptide's closest rival in the standings, and the victory clinched at least second place in the division for Riptide.
