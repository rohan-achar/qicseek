ICS 269, Winter 2000: Theory Seminar

5 May 2000:
Shape Transformation Using Variational Implicit Functions
Sergey Kirshner, ICS, UC Irvine

The paper by Greg Turk and James F.O'Brien introduces a method of creating a smooth interpolating function to build a morph between two 2D or 3D shapes.

As a side note, this paper is a base for the project I am working on with Prof. Renato Pajarola. If time permits, I will describe the direction of the project.
