ICS 261, Winter 2006:
Data Structures

This course meets Monday, Wednesday, and Friday, 3:00 - 3:50 in PSCB 230. The text is "Introduction to Algorithms" by Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, and Stein, however the lectures will also cover additional material not in that text. Coursework will consist of weekly homeworks (25%), a midterm (30%), and a comprehensive final exam (45%).

Homeworks will be assigned on Wednesdays, due in class the following Monday. Due to lack of teaching assistance for graduate classes, we will be employing the following homework grading policy: you must be present in class Monday for your homework to be scored. At the start of the lecture, I will go over the answers to the homeworks together with a scoring rubric, and each student will grade his or her own homework according to the rubric. I will then collect and record the graded homeworks.
