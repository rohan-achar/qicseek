Mitchell Park, I



This playground succeeds in breaking away from the boring safe uniformity of most playgrounds these days, with several interesting play structures including this one which appears to be Berliner Seilfabrik's Venus. We went there several times this trip even though it was not the closest playground. Someone at the park told us there are similar structures at another park on Meadow, on the other side of Middlefield, but we didn't get there this time.
