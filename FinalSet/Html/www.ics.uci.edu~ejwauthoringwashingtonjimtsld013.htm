Ordered Collections

  • Problem: protocol specification does not support ordering of collection members

    • Ordered collections are useful for modeling compound documents and version ordering

  • Proposed solution:

    • Add support for ordered collections to the protocol specification
    • Pro:
      • Makes certain document management applications "substantially easier" to build on top of WebDAV
    • Con:
      • Difficult for filesystem-based servers to implement ordered collections
      • Handling of multiple orderings is unknown
