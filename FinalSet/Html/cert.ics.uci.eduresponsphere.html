Responsphere

Responshere is an instrumented multisensor smartspace at UCI implementing & monitoring response activities.

Responsphere Testbed
Incident Level Response

300+ optical/acoustic sensors (Linksys, D-link, Canon) – fixed and mobile, pervasive 802.11, 4TB RAID, 64-bit 8 processor Solaris simulation server, 8 processor Windows 2003 server, 22-node Beowulf cluster, ZigBee motes, robotic platforms, people counters (fixed and mobile), GPS devices, RFID equipment, video equipment (CC extraction), multiple data sets.
