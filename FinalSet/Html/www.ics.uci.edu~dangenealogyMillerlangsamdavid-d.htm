Dear Moishe,

Yes, my brother's name is David and he was named for my grandfather (I 
should have included his name before). And yes, my mother's name is 
Esther. 

And yes, Harry Langsam was my grandfather's older brother. And yes, his 
offspring are our cousins and we know them well.  My grandfather had 
another brother, Morris, who married Rose (I think maiden name Fish). 
She was my grandmother's aunt. My grandmother was Flora Fish, and I think 
her mother's maiden name was Spira, which I see is a family name of other 
Langsam relatives. I know that my grandparents came from the same area in 
Europe.

How did Pesach Langsam take the surname "Langsam"?  You know that it is 
German for "slowly". Was your direct ancestor a Miller by profession? I know 
that that's how many surnames were adopted then.
