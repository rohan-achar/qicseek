Debra A. Brodbeck
 ISR Technical Relations Director

Debra A. Brodbeck is the primary contact point for the Institute.

Debra is the primary liaison with ISR sponsors. She oversees the direction and daily operations of ISR, and maintains the ISR information infrastructure. She is responsible for industry relations, interactions with similar organizations, and publicity of ISR activities. Debra has been part of ISR since 1991. She received her Master's degree in Computer Science from the University of Pittsburgh. She is a member of the ACM and an IEEE Computing Society affiliate.
