Riptide vs. Steal Sisters, first playoff

The longest game of the season, this turned into a pitching duel and ended in the seventh inning using international tie breaker rules (the inning starts with an extra runner on 2nd). Final score 6-4, so Riptide advances to the semifinal, but with a big disadvantage due to having played our best pitcher for six of her allowed seven innings already that day.
