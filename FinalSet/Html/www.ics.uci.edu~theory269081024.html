October 24, Fall 2008: Theory Seminar

1:00pm in 253 ICS

Two results on 3d lattice embedding of graphs

Prof. David Eppstein, UC Irvine

I'll present the results from two of my papers at Graph Drawing 2008: "Isometric diamond subgraphs", about embedding graphs into the three-dimensional diamond lattice, and "The topology of three-dimensional bendless orthogonal graph drawing", about embedding graphs into space in such a way that three mutually orthogonal edges meet at each vertex.
