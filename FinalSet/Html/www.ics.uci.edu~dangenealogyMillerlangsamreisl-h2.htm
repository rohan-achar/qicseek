The Reisel Adler nee Langsam Family


Reisel Langsam (Feb 20, 1864 - ?), daughter of Meilach and Golda Langsam of Kanczuga, Poland, was married to Isaac Adler, son of Eisig and Mindel of Panavolice. Meliach Langsam passed away before his daughter was actually born. This information comes from Polish records, as researched by Mr. Arye Barkai and Ms. Suzan Wynne. Reisel and Isaac had five known children.

  • Itta (Mar 25, 1886 - ?)
  • Hersh Meilech (Jul 19, 1887 - ?)
  • Leib (Jan 17, 1890 - ?)
  • Moses (Nov 22, 1895 - ?)
  • Sarah (Jan 19, 1901 - ?)
