Impact of Memory Stalls

  • A statically scheduled processor with a blocking cache stalls, on average, for

    • 15% of the time in integer benchmarks
    • 43% of the time in f.p. benchmarks
    • 70% of the time in transaction benchmark

  • Possible performance improvements due to improved memory hierarchy without technology scaling:

    • 1.17x,
    • 1.89x, and
    • 3.33x

  • Possible improvements with technology scaling

    • 2.4x, 7.5x, and 20x
