Retake sessions during finals week are

Monday 2, 3, 4, 5 pm

Tuesday 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 pm

Wednesday 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 pm

All session are in ICS 183.

Each session starts on the hour given, and lasts for 50 minutes.

You may take up to one retake for each exam during a session; for instance, you can retake Lab 1 and Lab 2 once each during the same session, but you cannot make two attempts at Lab 3 during the same session.

Please do not turn in an exam you know you will not pass; it just slows down our grading, without providing any new information to you. Please be very careful to label your exam project folder correctly, to include in the exam's files your name and ID where indicated, and to turn in the exam to the correct dropbox: we cannot grade what we do not have or cannot find!

We will grade tests as quickly as we can, given our resources. If you are worried whether you passed a test, and are worried that the grade may not be posted in time for you to retake it again if you need to, take the test again, even though your previous grade is not yet posted: if you pass a retake, we do not grade any subsequent retakes for that test, so retaking an 'extra' test will not hurt your grade.
