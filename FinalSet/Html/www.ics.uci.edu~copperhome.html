Compiler-Controlled Continuous Power-Performance Management

A DARPA/ITO Supported Project


Welcome to the COPPER Project Group at UCI. Our group consists of four faculty members and five graduate students. The goal of COPPER project is to build and demonstrate a capability in hardware (processor-memory) and software (compilers) for management of power resources and its tradeoff against speed, accuracy and throughput needs. For specific information, we welcome you to browse through our web pages.
