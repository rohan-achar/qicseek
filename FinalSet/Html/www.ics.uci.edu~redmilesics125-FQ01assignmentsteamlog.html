On-going Homework: Team Logs

Beginning with your first team meeting! (no later than October 8, 2001)

Maintain as a link or links from your team web page, or if necessary, on paper, a log of team meetings that contains the following elements:

  • Team Name
  • Date
  • Time Began
  • Time Ended
  • Attendance List
  • Purpose of the Meeting
  • Minutes of the Meeting
    • Topics Discussed
    • Open Issues
    • Questions Asked/Answered
    • Individual Assignments

If two or more sub-teams meet at the same time, make separate log sheets. Log sheets are expected to be about 1 to 2 pages. Most likely, the log will be in list form.
