The Moses Langsam Family


Moses Langsam lived in Bircza, into his 90's. This information comes from research by Ms. Estelle Guzik and Ms. Suzan Wynne. He had three known children.

  • Nathan (1872 - ?) came to the USA in 1906.
  • Chaim came to the USA before 1906.
  • Pesje (1888 - ?) came to the USA in 1906.
