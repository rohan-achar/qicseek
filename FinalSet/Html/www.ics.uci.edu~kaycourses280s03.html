Computer Law Seminar -- Course Reference


Instructor: David G. Kay, 406B Computer Science. Office hours: Officially Tuesdays and Thursdays after class, but feel free to drop by any time or send electronic mail to kay.

Readings: I'll assign readings as we go. Some will be on-line; others will be available in the ICS grad course handout file, in the filing cabinet outside the ICS kitchen.
