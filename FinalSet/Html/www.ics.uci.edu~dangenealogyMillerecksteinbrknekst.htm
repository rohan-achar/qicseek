Sandy married Melvin Persily and have Marc, Eric & Karen. Marc is married to Robin Workman and they have Jacob Evan & Jesse Benjamin. Dr. Eric Persily, an anesthesiologist in private practice in Charleston WV, is married to Dr. Cynthia Armstrong Persily <cpersily@wvu.edu>. She continues to hold the position of chair for the Charleston division. They have two children, Aaron David, born October 13, 1995, and Alexandra Beth, born October 21, 1997.
