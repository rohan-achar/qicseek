Consequences

AOP has the following benefits:

  1. They maintain application modularity.
  2. They separate the specifications of aspects.

AOP has the following limitations:

  1. Interactions between aspects
  2. They can complicate debugging. Debugging an application that contains complex interactions between aspects arise can be difficult.
