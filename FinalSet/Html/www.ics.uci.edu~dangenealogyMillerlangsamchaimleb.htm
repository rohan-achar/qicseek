The Chaim Leib Langsam Family


Chaim Leib (c.1783- 1866), son of Pesach Langsam, married Chana, a young woman who was an orphan. They had 7 known children. Chaim Leib was able to obtain some land which he farmed successfully. This enabled him to further increase his holdings. and become known as a gentleman farmer. Of note is the fact that quite a few of his grandchildren bore the names of the Bnei Yissoschur, Tzvi Elimelech, and his wife, Chana Mindel. Perhaps Chaim Leib's wife was a descendant (daughter or granddaughter).

  • Menachem Mendel (c.1820 - 1915) of Bukowsko, Galicia, married 3 times; to Ita, Perla Lea, & Sara. There were children from all three wives. He had a tavern with an inn in Bukowsko and was known as Roiter (Red) Mendel, due to his red hair.
