Dear Moishe

Thank you for your email.  Charlotte Arlt was my great-aunt who married
Alfred Langsam in Krakow around the early 1930's.
I don't know where Alfred was born but I believe he is from Galicia.  Alfred
had one brother (name unknown, maybe Henry) and one sister (Hilda - who
married a Bronstein). Hilda had one daughter - her name is Ruth.  She would
probably be close to 60 years of age today and I understand she has two
sons.  I do not know Ruth's married name.

Regarding the offspring of Charlotte and Alfred - Charlotte and Alfred had
twin boys.  One has passed away and the other, with whom I am in seldom
contact.  I am not sure if he would be interested in pursuing his family
history but I will mention you to him when I next speak with him (could be
some time).  I will try to find out more info for you if you like.

Shalom
Anita
