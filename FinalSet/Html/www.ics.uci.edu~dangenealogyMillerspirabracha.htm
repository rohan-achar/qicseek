The Pesach Moshe and Bracha Weinberger Family


Pesach Moshe Weinberger married Bracha, descendent of R' Hersh Mylech Spira, the Bnei Yissoschur. They lived in Dynow and Bukowsko, Poland at some point in time. Pesach Moshe and Bracha had four known children.

  • Nechemia (1863 - 1960) was born in Dynow, Poland. He married Sara Kelner and lived in the Przemysl area of Galica, Poland. They had seven known children. Around 1900 the family move to Cologne, Germany. Later in life Nechemia moved to Israel and is buried in Bnei Brak.
