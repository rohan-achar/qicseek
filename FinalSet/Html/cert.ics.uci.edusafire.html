Situational Awareness for Fire Fighters

SAFIRE (funding by DHS / FEMA) utilzes next generation information technology to bring situational awareness to Incident Commanders. We make use of a variety of sensors (e.g., CO, acuostic, motes), networks, and visualization capabilities to provide information to and from first responders.
