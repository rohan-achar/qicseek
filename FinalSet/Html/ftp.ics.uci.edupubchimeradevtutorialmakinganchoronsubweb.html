Making an Anchor on the Subweb

  You can utilize the inherited Anchors and Links on the new subweb. To demonstrate, we will add a new anchored image to the inherited link.

Making an Anchor on a new Image

Download the image yeager_star.jpeg from the following site:
Chimera 2.0 Turorial Download page
Place it anywhere you like.

Display the image using Jimage.

Make an anchor on the image and add it to the Active Link (x1).

Demonstrating that the inherited Anchors Exist

Now do a link traversal on the anchor in yeager_star.jpeg by clicking inside the anchor area using the left mouse button. The two other images of the X-1 will appear with their anchors flashing.
