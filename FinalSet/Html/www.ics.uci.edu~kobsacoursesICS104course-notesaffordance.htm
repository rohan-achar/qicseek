Affordances

"term that refers to the properties of objects -- what sorts of operations and manipulations can be done to a particular object" (D. A. Norman 1988, The Psychology of everyday things).

  • A door affords opening
  • A chair affords support

"perceived affordance": the extent to which objects suggest their affordance.

 

   User interface elements should be designed in such a way that they clearly indicate their affordances.
